About Nava

Nava Dunkelman is a percussionist and improviser based in the Bay Area. She is a founding member of the experimental music collective Dapplegray and has performed with various ensembles and solo projects.

Her work explores the boundaries between composition and improvisation, often incorporating extended techniques and unconventional approaches to percussion instruments.

Nava has collaborated with artists across various disciplines and has performed at venues and festivals throughout the United States and internationally.

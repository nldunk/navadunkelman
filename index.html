Bio

Nava Dunkelman is a percussionist, composer, and collaborator. Recent performances include Roulette (NYC), Monheim Triennale (DE), and projects with Du Yun’s Ok Miss. Nava’s work blends acoustic and electronic textures to sculpt immersive rhythmic narratives.
